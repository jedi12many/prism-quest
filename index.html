⚙️ Menu

🚶 Tap the ground to walk (or WASD/arrows on desktop).

⛏️ Tap a sparkling gem node to walk over and mine it. Nodes regrow after a minute.

🪞 Polish raw minerals in your Bag — stop the slider in the bright center for Brilliant quality.

✨ Craft spells from polished gems in the Spellbook. Each craft gives limited charges.

⚔️ Walk into monsters to battle. Defeat them for XP and gem drops.

🏰 Your camp is a sunny safe zone — gloom-things cannot step into sunshine, and resting there heals you. Build the Kitchen, Unicorn Stalls, Training Grounds and more with raw minerals to grow your power.

🌳✨ Studying takes peace and quiet: the Power Tree and Spellbook only open at camp.

🐉 The Rainwyrm guards the Sunken Gate in the dark corner. Beyond it waits Sog'naroth, the Endless Drizzle — defeat it to bring back the sun. Sunlight spells burn it; its whispers bring Dread.

Progress saves automatically on this device.
