💎 Prism Quest 🌈

The world of Rainyday has spent a hundred years beneath one endless storm. Deep in the gloom squirms Sog'naroth, the Endless Drizzle — an ancient thing that can only exist where the sun never shines.

From the village of Drizzlewick: quest into the wilds, defeat the gloom champions, ride a rainbow to the Rainycastle, and bring back the sunshine!

🏰 Your Camp

⚙️ Menu

🚶 Tap the ground to walk (or WASD/arrows on desktop). Tap villagers to talk — they drive the story.

🏘️ Drizzlewick is your home: always sunny, always safe. Rest to heal, build up your camp, and open the Power Tree and Spellbook here. The south gate leads to the wilds.

🌧️ The wilds of Rainyday redraw themselves every trip. Four gloom champions rule its regions — defeat one and sunlight floods that region for good.

⛏️ Tap gem nodes to mine. Polish all in your Bag turns raw minerals into gems (luck-based — the Factory and dwarf crews ⛏️ raise the odds). Gems fuel spells, camp upgrades, AND item sockets.

🧍 Character sheet: monsters drop randomized loot (Common → Magic → Rare → Legendary → Set). Equip 5 slots — Weapon, Helm, Armor, Boots, Charm — to boost your attack, defense, HP, crit, dodge, and more. Facet polished gems into item sockets for permanent power, and collect the Rainbow Raiment set for big 3-piece and 5-piece bonuses. Salvage what you don't need.

⛰️ Watch for dungeon entrances hidden in each zone — Gloom Caves, Sunken Ruins, and Haunted Houses. Each is a real interior 2–3 floors deep, getting deadlier as you descend. The stairs down are locked 🔒 — defeat the floor's Warden 🗝️ for the key. The keeper of the final floor guards legendary loot. Climb back out at any entrance; die and it's permanent.

🌈 Restore all four regions and the Rainycastle rises in the rainclouds. Step onto the plaza Cloudgate to ride your unicorn up and face the Rainwyrm.

🌀 Beyond the wyrm, a portal leads to Sog'naroth's realm — one way only: no village, no gems, just the skills and charges you carry. Win, and the sun returns forever.

💀 One life. If you fall, your hero is gone for good and you begin a new one. Fights get harder the further out you push (North is deadliest). Flee when you're outmatched, rest at camp, and take a revive capstone deep in your Power Tree for one cheat-death per life.

🌳 Level cap 12. You can't learn every skill — you'll only reach two of your three branch capstones. Build deliberately.

Progress saves automatically on this device.
