⚙️ Menu

🚶 Tap the ground to walk (or WASD/arrows on desktop).

⛏️ Tap a sparkling gem node to walk over and mine it. Nodes regrow after a minute.

🪞 Polish raw minerals in your Bag — stop the slider in the bright center for Brilliant quality.

✨ Craft spells from polished gems in the Spellbook. Each craft gives limited charges.

⚔️ Walk into monsters to battle. Defeat them for XP and gem drops.

🌳 Spend skill points in your Power Tree each time you level up.
